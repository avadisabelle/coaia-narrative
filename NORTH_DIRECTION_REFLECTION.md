**What We Learned**:
Engineer-world can process events alone. Ceremony-world can assess alone. Story-engine can narrate alone.

**But together, they create something impossible separately**:
- Engineer's precision grounded in ceremony's wisdom becomes *reliable integrity*
- Ceremony's wisdom implemented through engineer's precision becomes *actionable care*
- Story's narrative informed by both becomes *teaching that teaches*

**Teaching for Next Builders**:
